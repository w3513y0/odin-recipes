Mexican Tinga

Description:

Tinga is a Mexican dish made of shredded chicken cooked in a flavourful tomato-adobo sauce. It's often served on tostadas.

Ingredients

  • Onion and Oil
  • Can of stewed tomatoes and chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • Chicken
  • Tostada shells
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook the onion.
  2. Puree the tomatoes and chipotle peppers in a blender.
  3. Add the puree to the onions on the stove, stir in the chicken and simmer.
  4. Dollop the chicken mixture onto tostadas and top with sour cream
